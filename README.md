## 💡 Overview

<a href="#celemonyMelodyneStudio">Celemony Melodyne Studio</a> serves vocal producers, mixing engineers, music producers, and session engineers who need professional-grade pitch and timing correction for vocal performances, and the creative pitch manipulation capabilities that modern music production requires.

The <a href="#celemonyMelodyneStudio">vocal production use case</a> is Melodyne's primary application — correcting intonation in recorded vocal performances while maintaining the natural character and expression of the performance. Melodyne's note-level editing provides the surgical precision to fix specific notes while leaving the surrounding performance intact, and the formant control maintains vocal character when pitch-shifting would otherwise make the voice sound unnatural. The <a href="#celemonyMelodyneStudio">harmony creation use case</a> generates harmony parts from a lead vocal — shifting the pitch of a copy of the lead vocal to create harmonies that follow the original performance's phrasing and timing, building vocal arrangements from a single recorded performance.

The <a href="#celemonyMelodyneStudio">polyphonic instrument editing use case</a> applies DNA Direct Note Access to recorded chord instruments — adjusting individual chord voicings after recording, changing the harmony of a guitar part without re-recording, or restructuring the chord progression of a piano performance. The <a href="#celemonyMelodyneStudio">creative pitch and time manipulation use case</a> uses Melodyne's tools for intentionally creative effects — extreme pitch shifting for vocal effects, time-based manipulation for rhythmic creativity, and formant shifting for tonal character transformation beyond natural performance range.</p>

## 🙋 FAQ

<details>
<summary>What is formant control in Melodyne?</summary>

Formant control shifts the formant frequencies — the resonant character of a voice or instrument — independently from the musical pitch, maintaining natural sound quality when pitch-shifting beyond natural range.

</details>

<details>
<summary>How does Melodyne differ from Auto-Tune?</summary>

Melodyne provides note-level blob editing for precise manual correction with DNA polyphonic capability; Auto-Tune focuses on automatic and real-time pitch correction with the characteristic auto-tune effect when used aggressively. Both correct pitch; their workflows and capabilities differ significantly.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Does Melodyne have time stretching?</summary>

Yes. Time stretching adjusts the duration of audio events with Melodyne's high-quality algorithm.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Can Melodyne operate without a DAW?</summary>

Yes. Standalone application mode provides a complete pitch editing environment without requiring a DAW.

</details>
